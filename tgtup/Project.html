PROJECT

Passion Project

TGTUP teamed-up with Passion Projects in the ambitious goal of enriching more than 470 students in Mayoyao, Ifugao. Aside from helping provide school materials and workshop kits to students of three remotely located schools, TGTUP delivered the message of environmental responsibility to around 100 students at Patyay Elementary School.

Rehabilitation of Lan-Ag Elementary School

TGTUP participated with the reconstruction of Lan-Ag Elementary School on 17th-18th of December 2016, which was affected by the recent Typhoon Lawin in Lacub, Abra.

TGTUP Build: Radiate

TGT|UP|BUILD Volunteer with the Indigineous communities for the Sustainable Development Goals, climate action and life on land respectively.

Mission ABaKaDa

Mission ABaKaDa is TGTUP’s fundraising initiative that brings the message that anyone can take action to help augment the needs for continuous learning of our adopted schools. The amount will be used to purchase printers, bond papers, radios, headsets, or others depending on the identified needs per school.
