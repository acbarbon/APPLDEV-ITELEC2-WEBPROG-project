    • THE GREEN THUMBS UP PROJECT

    TGTUP is geared towards motivating, mobilizeing, and mobilizing kids for environmental leadership. This responds to United Nation's Sustainable Development Goals Nos. 13 and 15, Climate Action and Life on Land respectively.
    Learn more

    The 17 proposed SDGs include SDG 13: “Take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts”.

    Forests are vitally important for sustaining life on Earth, and play a major role in the fight against climate change.

    TGTUP's
    MISSION

    MOBILIZE
    TGTUP promotes collaboration amongthe youth in thespirit of Cooperativism.

    It focuses on establishing good relationship and camaraderie towards
    a bright future of aspiring young leaders.
    MOLD
    TGTUP shapes and hones
    personalities to prepare the youth
    for excellent leadership.

    It enriches the youth with knowledge concerning environmental care and ethics.
    MOTIVATE
    TGTUP entices the youth to partake
    in community service and
    environmental activities.

    It also delivers outreach programs towards people who are in need.
